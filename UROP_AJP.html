Aerosol Jet Printing on Paper-like Substract for Electromagnetism Application



Team: Dr. Huang Shaoying, Dr. Alvin Tan, Manish, Ayush, Kizhakke Chittadath, Yoong Hao, Yu Quan

Description:
In this research, we aim to investigate printing of conductive ink in various paper substrates using aerosol jet printing. We have also did a literature review as a team to better understand, as well as various experiments to optimise the current printing process.

Responsibility (Research Assistant):

  • Literature review of aerosol-jet printing and inkjet printing
  • Dimensionless Analysis
  • Operating of aersol jet printer to prepare sample
  • Operating FESEM for material characterisation
  • Attempt to create a model to understand AJP with Ansys Fluent

    • Skills Involved: Material Characterisation(FESEM), 3D Printing(AJP), Modeling, Lab Work
