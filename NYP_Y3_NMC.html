Elixir-N

Team Members: Qing Hong, Swee Kian, Syukri and Yu Quan

Project Description:
Our team have develop this anti-bacterial slime that allow users to clean themselves for situation where usage of water is not possible. This includes patients that is bedridden and soldiers out in the field. The slime have a cooling sensation, which mimic the cooling sensation after a shower. We also incoporate a small amount of nanosilver, which give the slime its antibacterial properties.

Materials:
PVOH, Sodium Borohydride, Nanosilver
