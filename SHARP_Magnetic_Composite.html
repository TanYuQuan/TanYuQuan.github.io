3D Printing of Magnetic Composite

Team: Dr. Low Hong Yee, Ri Han, Yu Quan

Description:
[Ongoing] In this research, we are looking ways to improve the performance of 3D printed magnetic composite. Currently, I am assisting with an ongoing research project with DSO, but will be pivoting to my own project. As of now, for my project, we are exploring using FDM with different polymer as a matrix in hope to increase the loading of magnetic material.

Responsibility (Research Assistant):

  • Literature review on application and making of 3D printed magnetic composite
  • Assist in characterisation of sample, such as FESEM and TGA
  • Assist in making of raw materials for printing
  • Assist in 3D printing of sample
  • Doing my own research project
