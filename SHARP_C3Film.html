Surface Texturing for Carbon Capture

Team: Dr. Low Hong Yee, Dr. Tan Mei Chee, Kay Jin, Daniel, Kush, Beverly, Yu Quan

Description:
In this research, the aim is to investigate the C3 film that is developed by the other members of the research team, as well as exploring options to improve the carbon capture process.

Responsibility (Research Assistant):

  • FESEM on the sample
  • Usage of goniometer to characterise the sample made
  • Proposed a texture design that maximise the surface area for carbon capture

    • Skills Involved: Design Thinking, Lab Work, Material Characterisation (FESEM, Goniometer)
